IMD Agromet Advisory - District: Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu
Issued: Valid for next 5 days

Weather Forecast:
Maximum temperature is likely to range between 36 to 38 degrees Celsius during the next five days.
Minimum temperature will hover around 24 to 26 degrees Celsius.
Heavy rainfall of about 75 mm is expected on day 3 and day 4 due to a low pressure system over the Bay of Bengal.
Relative humidity is expected to remain high at around 82 percent in the morning hours.
Wind speed of 35 to 45 km/hr is forecasted from the south-westerly direction during the rainfall period.

Advisory:
Farmers are advised to take precautionary measures against possible water logging in low-lying paddy fields.
Spraying of pesticides and fertilizers should be postponed until the rainfall subsides.
Provide mechanical support to standing banana and sugarcane crops to prevent lodging due to strong winds.
Monitor crops for fungal infections, especially in tomato and chili, due to high humidity.
